About Me

Greetings! I am Allen Velazco or 'Arien', a passionate and versatile technical artist and game developer.

At 19 years old (2004), I'm deeply passionate about both art and game programming. My skill set encompasses a wide range of expertise, including shader programming, VFX, environment art and gameplay programming. I'm proud to have the ability to be able to seamlessly integrate both artistic and technical elements of game development.
In the 4 years I've done game development, I've had the pleasure of conributing to a few small indie games such as:
Cataplorers, where I did most of the gameplay mechanics.
Blessed, where I made all the shaders and VFX.
High Command, where I created custom PBR shaders, VFX and contributed to environment and level design.
